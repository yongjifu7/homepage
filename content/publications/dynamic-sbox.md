A novel S-box construction scheme using a two-dimensional chaotic map combined with irreducible polynomials over $\mathrm{GF}(2^8)$. Ablation studies verify strong cryptographic properties (nonlinearity, SAC, BIC) and high generation efficiency; encryption experiments confirm strong pixel-level confusion and resistance to differential / statistical attacks.

新颖的 S 盒构造方案，采用二维混沌映射并在 $\mathrm{GF}(2^8)$ 域中结合不可约多项式。消融实验验证其满足非线性度、SAC、BIC 等密码学指标并具备高生成效率；图像加密实验进一步证明其在像素扰乱与抗差分、统计攻击上的优异表现。
