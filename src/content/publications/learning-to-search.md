A cognitively-inspired framework that couples a learned neural solver with adaptive search for large-scale Vehicle Routing Problems (VRPs). The solver learns to guide the search, and the search traces in turn provide high-quality supervision for the solver — a tight "learning-to-search, searching-to-learn" loop that scales to instances far beyond prior end-to-end neural methods.

认知启发的神经求解框架，通过"学习指导搜索、搜索反哺学习"的耦合循环，有效处理超大规模 VRP 问题，显著超越以往端到端神经求解器的可扩展性。
